# AQI-Dashboard
1. Decoding Air Quality: An Analytical Study of AQI Trends
A data-driven, analytical, and interactive dashboard built to evaluate AQI trends over time to compare the level of AQI and the distribution of pollutants(PM2.5, PM10, CO2, etc.), regionally, to guide priorities in future decision making and support environmental awareness.
2. Air pollution is a global environmental problem that mostly influences the health of the urban and rural population. Over the past few decades, epidemiological studies have consistently demonstrated adverse health effects associated with higher ambient levels of air pollution. The World Health Organization (WHO) estimates that 25% of all deaths in the developing world can be directly attributed to environmental factors (WHO, 2006). The problem of air pollution and its corresponding adverse health impacts have been aggravated due to increasing industrial and other developmental activities.
